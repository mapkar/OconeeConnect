Welcome to OconeeConnect

OconeeConnect is a growing, community-driven Meshtastic network covering Oconee County, South Carolina. Starting in Seneca (home to about 9,200 residents) and expanding toward Walhalla and the surrounding foothills, we're building a reliable mesh for text messaging, GPS location sharing, and emergency alerts. Perfect for hiking the Blue Ridge trails, boating on Lake Keowee, rural living, or when storms knock out power and cell service.

Powered by affordable LoRa devices and open-source Meshtastic software, our network is decentralized, encrypted, and works peer-to-peer over long distances—often miles between nodes, with solar-powered repeaters extending coverage.

Long Range & Reliable

Messages hop from node to node, covering rural areas where cell service drops. Ideal for Oconee's lakes, forests, and mountains.

Private & Secure

AES-256 encryption keeps your chats private. No accounts, no tracking—just local communication.

Low Power, Low Cost

Devices run for days on a battery (or forever with solar). Hardware starts around $30–50.

Community Built

Join hundreds in upstate SC meshes. Help expand by hosting a node or donating for repeaters on high ground.
